each advise multiple customers holistically on different technologies and
architectures in various cloud and container environments. If you enjoy working
with bleeding-edge technologies every day and thrive in public, private, and
hybrid cloud environments, bespinian is the right place for you!

- You have practical experience with Kubernetes, Terraform, AWS, Azure, or GCP

- You like to work with multiple customers and technologies
- You are interested in participating in shaping the company and taking
  responsibility
- You are currently located in or are willing to relocate to Switzerland
- You are fluent in German and English

## Nice to Have

- You have experience with microservice architectures
