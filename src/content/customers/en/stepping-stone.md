## Customer

[stepping stone AG](https://www.stepping-stone.ch/) is a Swiss IT services
company based in Bern, founded in 2004. The company specializes in engineering
and automation of IT services with a strong focus on open-source solutions.
stepping stone AG operates its own Swiss cloud infrastructure, stoney cloud,
distributed across two data centers, offering scalable and highly available
solutions. Their services include managed cloud offerings such as backup,
monitoring, maintenance, and on-call support for small and medium-sized
businesses in Switzerland.

## Background

As a cloud provider with deep roots in open-source technologies such as
OpenStack, Linux, and KVM, stepping stone AG recognized the growing importance
of container technologies and Kubernetes in the modern IT landscape. With the
recent expansion of their offering to include Container-as-a-Service on their
stoney cloud platform, the team wanted to deepen their expertise in container
orchestration to better serve their customers and operate their own
infrastructure more effectively.

A further initiative was the deployment of Jitsi, an open-source video
conferencing solution, on their Kubernetes clusters. This required not only
technical implementation knowledge, but also a solid understanding of security
best practices and stability aspects for running production workloads on
Kubernetes.

## Project Goal

The collaboration had the following objectives:

- Build advanced Kubernetes knowledge within the stepping stone team
- Enable the team to independently operate and troubleshoot Kubernetes clusters
- Successfully deploy Jitsi on their Kubernetes infrastructure using Helm
- Ensure the deployment meets security and stability requirements for production
  use

## bespinian's Role

bespinian delivered a comprehensive training and consulting engagement over four
half-days.

### Container and Kubernetes Training

We conducted a structured training covering container fundamentals, Kubernetes
usage, and operational best practices. The sessions were designed to build
knowledge incrementally and enable the stepping stone team to work confidently
with containerized workloads and understand the underlying concepts of
Kubernetes architecture.

### Jitsi Helm Chart Deployment

In hands-on collaboration with the stepping stone team, we supported the
installation and configuration of the Jitsi Helm chart on one of their clusters.
This practical exercise reinforced the training content while delivering
immediate business value.

### Security and Stability Review

We conducted a review of their Kubernetes setup with a focus on security
hardening and operational stability. This included analysis of network policies,
resource limits, pod security configurations, and other aspects critical for
reliably running production workloads.

## Technologies Used

- **Container Platform**: Kubernetes
- **Deployment**: Helm
- **Infrastructure**: stoney cloud (OpenStack-based)
